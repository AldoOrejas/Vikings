OUR HISTORY

 Viking Line, Inc. dba Viking Trailways was established in 1972 by Gale and Beverly Webb.  From the start, attention to customer service and satisfaction was to be job one.  Prompt on time delivery of customers in clean well maintained equipment has continued to this day.  

LONG TERM BUSINESS RELATIONSHIPS

What Viking Trailways tries to stress is that sometimes a customer needs to realize the type of service they receive for the price of a trip.  When you consider all the extras that Viking Trailways provides, does it make sense to book your trip with a company that has less to offer?  After nearly 40 years of service Viking Trailways is still owned and operated by its founders, also the only bus company in the area that can say that. 

Viking Trailways operates the newest fleet of buses in the area.  Each bus is equipped with GPS systems giving parents, guardians, or group leaders the opportunity to monitor the location and safety of buses and passengers.  Since October of 2005 Viking Trailways has been Department of Defense Safety Certified, an award that no other bus company in the area has been awarded.

Viking Trailways also allows the Department of Transportation to use its shop to train their inspectors.  With that in mind it's no wonder that Viking Trailways has the highest D.O.T safety rating in the area.  In fall of 2009, Viking Trailways was the only bus company in the area to meet strict guidelines and was selected as an official bus supplier for the 2010 Winter Olympics in Vancouver, BC.
